The learning cycle

From Curiosity to Creation

A repeatable journey students run on every mission — building the habit of learning how to learn.

01

Research & Understand

Students observe, ask questions, and understand real-world problems.

02

Make

Students build working models using robotics, electronics, coding, and engineering kits.

03

Innovate

Students test, fail, repair, improve, and adapt their solution.

04

Reflect

Students explain what worked, what changed, and what skill they used.
